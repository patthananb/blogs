About Me

Early Life

I was born Patthanan Bean Bhandhumanee on September 28, 2001, in Saraburi, Thailand, and grew up in Trang. Three years after I was born, I have a sister named Bam. My grandmother and my mom raised me up in Trang.

Education

I studied kindergarten and elementary school at Buranalumuk School. When I turned 15, I moved to Bangkok to study for my Vocational Certificate degree in Electrical Technician at King Mongkut's University of Technology North Bangkok (KMUTNB). After that, I continued studying at the same university for my Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering. I didn't do well in university and took 6 years to finish my degree.
